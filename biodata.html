Colouring

My second hobby is colouring even thought I am not very fluent with my drawing skill, but I do enjoy colouring. Today, there are lots of adult colouring books that allow me to freely colour the image either by using pencil colours, watercolour, crayons and so on. Not only that, nowadays there are also painting by numbers on canvas that are sold via offline and online shop, where I can colour the picture that I want by using the watercolor that have been given. The uniqueness of this canvas painting by number is the watercolor have different numbers for different colour which these colours is represent the colours that used in the picture. Therefore, I can simply colouring the picture according to the numbers which helps me to feel calm and not feel pressured in thinking of what colour that I should use to make the picture looks interesting because all the colours have been set already. Not to forget, this canvas painting by numbers also have their own level consisting of beginners level (small size of canvassing), intermediate level (medium size of canvas) and lastly is advanced level (big size of canvas). Thus, I highly recommend you to choose the small one if you are trying this canvas painting by numbers for the first time, because the bigger the canvas, the more small elements to colour which I personally think this might hard for some beginners.
