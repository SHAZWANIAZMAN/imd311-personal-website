Credit to: NurAfifah/@lubukplanner

First of all, on September 2020, I started my business journey as a dropship of planner books 2021 in which the brand of the books called as Syabab Malaysia. There are 3 types of planner which is exclusive, standard and student planners. Each of it have a differences in terms of price, number of freegifts, contents, features and more. At first, I know this existence of planners brand is from my cousin, where she was an agent of Syabab planners since 2019. Thus, I decided to be one of her dropships which the name of her online shop is known as Lubuk Planner. After that, I started to create my second Twitter account for business purposes and from this platform is where I gained a lot of other sellers friends. Thus, we decided to help each other by liking, retweet their business's tweet. Fortunately, although this is my first trying in businesss but I managed to achieved my business goals so I am proud of myself. Credit to: Atikah/@kakfit

Not only planners and calendars, I am also a dropship of Kakfit brand which is a personalised thermos, tumbler and bottle that allows customers to custom their name using font colours and font types that have been provided. I started to join this dropship in about February 2020 until now.
